## Data sources and accuracy

GEFO synthesises public datasets (UN Comtrade, World Bank, AIS samples, Natural Earth, etc.) and proprietary computed indicators. **We make no warranty about accuracy, completeness, or timeliness.** Sources are referenced in our analytical briefs and in [PRIVACY.md](./PRIVACY.md).

In particular:
- Shipping density and vessel positions are **delayed** by default, not real-time.
- Trade data is from the most recent complete annual reporting cycle, which is **typically 12–18 months behind**.
- Proprietary indicators rely on simplified routing assumptions documented in the codebase — they are heuristics, not measurements.

**Do not use GEFO as the sole basis for financial, trading, or operational decisions.**
